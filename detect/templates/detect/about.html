Why Did I choose this project?

The presence of COVID-19 is still felt by everyone of us, meaning we must continue to fight against the virus to prevent further loss of lives.

Masks continue to play a vital role in protecting people, being 79% effective in preventing transmission.

A mask detector application can be used in crowded areas or in public services to promote the wearing of face masks and thus reduce COVID transmission, detailed in this research paper.

What does it do and what does it use?

I wanted it to predict via a livestream with the camera whether a person is wearing a mask, not wearing a mask, or wearing a mask incorrectly. However it fails at the latter since it is too hard to differentiate. The website you're looking at is built with Django, and the machine learning part is done with Keras, whilst I used OpenCV in order to get a live video feed and detect the faces.

I hit several challenges with training the model since I used a relatively small kaggle dataset.

There is much room for improvement in the future especially with the AI part.
